Say goodbye to homeland

Growing up in China, I have experienced Chinese-style primary school, middle school and high school. In the summer of 2018, I got the opportunity to study abroad. After a year of online classes, I got on a plane to Canada in 2022 and said goodbye to my family.

Ottawa

My school is located in Ottawa, the capital of Canada. Although it is the capital city, the decoration of the city is not as prosperous as the neighboring Toronto and Montreal. But Ottawa makes me feel very comfortable, it's very leisurely to walk down the street every day, it's a slow city just like I like it.

It is October now, which is the most beautiful period of maple leaves. Whenever I sit by the window, I can see the beautiful maple trees. Besides, the evening sun in Ottawa is also beautiful.

The college life

Now I am in my second year of university life, and I have experienced the first year of online course teaching. I am looking forward to the offline course very much. When I came to the campus, the fact also gave me a positive response, having the opportunity to communicate with classmates and teachers face to face, which made my college life more rich.
