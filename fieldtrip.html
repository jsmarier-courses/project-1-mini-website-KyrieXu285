The Champlain Lookout

We had an interesting field trip last week. We followed Professor Mrs. Roxanne from GEG1301 to The Champlain Lookout, which is located on top of the Eardley Escarpment.

We took the school bus to this place. After 30 minutes' drive, we reached the top of the mountain. As soon as I got off the bus, the wind from the top of the mountain was oncoming, and the scenery in front of me was even more beautiful.

The loop

The professor told us about how the terrain was formed, and also about the kinds of stones in the area. Then we began to explore the trail. The path will go down from the roadside steps, and the whole journey is 3 kilometers, which makes me tired after not exercising for a long time, but the scenery along the way makes me excited.

Although this activity was more like relaxing and hiking for me, I also learned a lot from it.
